Dropshipping StoreDevelopment Package

Dropshipping is a business model that allows you to sell products to customers from your online store without having to any physical keep stock on hand. Instead, the orders you receive are automatically forwarded to the supplier who processes and ships the product directly from their warehouse to your customer - all with a few simple clicks of a button!

Our Dropshipping Store Package is an inexpensive way for you to start your very own online business. We are here to develop your very own customised store from top to bottom, ready for you to start making sales!

How It Works

Sale price : $120

A customer purchases a product from your online store.

Supplier price : $50

With a few clicks your store forwards the order onto your supplier.

Tracking included

The supplier processes the order and posts it to your customer

Your profit: $70

You keep the profit!

Manging Your Website

Making changes and managing your website is done through a content management system. The two CMS's we use are Wordpress and Wix. Your choice of which CMS to use should be based on your technical experience. We provide training and support for which ever CMS you decide on.

Wix is perfect for those with little to no technical experience at all. It's intuitive user interface and features provides you the ability to manage and edit your website without any technical complexities or coding experience required. To put is this way, if you can use Facebook then you will have no problem managing your website using Wix.

Wordpress is a popular open source CMS used more favourable by those with technical and coding experience. Without experience you may run into a lot of trouble managing your website.
